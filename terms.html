1. About This Application

The drama.ohne.filter Content Tool ("the Tool", "we", "us") is a content publishing utility designed for short-form video and slide-based storytelling creators on TikTok. It enables authorized users to automate the upload of multi-slide image content to TikTok as drafts, which the creator then reviews, edits, and publishes through the TikTok app.

The Tool integrates with the TikTok Content Posting API to submit content to a creator's TikTok draft inbox. No content is published directly without the creator's manual review and approval inside the TikTok application.
