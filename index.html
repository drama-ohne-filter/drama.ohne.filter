A publishing utility for German-speaking short-form content creators on TikTok. Automate the upload of multi-slide image carousels as drafts — then add music, review, and publish directly inside the TikTok app.

Draft Upload

Content is submitted to your TikTok draft inbox via the Content Posting API. Nothing is published without your manual review.

Slide Formats

Optimized for multi-slide narrative content — relationship stories, personal growth, and creator storytelling formats.

Creator Control

Add music, captions, and hashtags in the TikTok app before publishing. Full editorial control stays with the creator.

Secure by Design

OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to third-party servers outside of TikTok's own API.
