Privacy Policy

drama.ohne.filter Content Tool  ·  Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the drama.ohne.filter Content Tool ("the Tool", "we", "us") handles information when you use the Tool to upload content to TikTok via the TikTok Content Posting API.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Policy explains what data we access, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Information We Access

When you authorize the Tool via TikTok Login, we access the following data through the TikTok API:

  • user.info.basic — Your TikTok Open ID, display name, and avatar URL. This is used solely to verify that the correct TikTok account is connected to the Tool.
  • video.upload — The ability to upload image content to your TikTok account as a draft. No content is published without your explicit action inside the TikTok app.

3. How We Use Your Data

Data accessed via the TikTok API is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account for upload requests.
  • Submit image content to your TikTok draft inbox.
  • Confirm successful upload via the API response.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics, or any purpose beyond the direct operation of the upload workflow described above.

4. Data Storage

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens issued by TikTok are stored locally on your device in a configuration file. These tokens are never transmitted to any server operated by us. No personal data, TikTok profile information, or content is stored on external servers by the Tool.

Uploaded image files are temporarily hosted on GitHub Pages solely for the purpose of providing TikTok's API with accessible URLs during the upload process. These temporary files are automatically deleted after the upload is complete.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or TikTok credentials with any third party, except as required to interact with the TikTok API as part of the Tool's core function. Your data is not shared with advertisers or analytics providers.

6. Third-Party Services

The Tool interacts with the following third-party services:

  • TikTok (ByteDance Ltd.) — Content is uploaded via the TikTok Content Posting API. TikTok's own Privacy Policy applies to data processed by TikTok's servers.
  • GitHub Pages — Used for temporary hosting of image files during upload. GitHub's Privacy Statement applies.

7. Data Retention

OAuth tokens stored locally on your device are retained until you revoke access via TikTok's app settings or manually delete the token file. Temporary upload files on GitHub Pages are deleted automatically after each upload session.

8. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Revoke the Tool's access to your TikTok account via TikTok's app settings under Settings → Privacy → Apps and Websites.
  • Delete locally stored token files from your device to remove all stored credentials.

9. Security

OAuth tokens are stored locally and never transmitted to third-party servers operated by us. We recommend keeping your device secure and not sharing token files with others.

10. Children's Privacy

This Tool is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the Tool after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us via TikTok Direct Message at @drama.ohne.filter.
